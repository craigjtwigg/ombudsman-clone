Hello, we're Ombudsman
Services.

We resolve disputes between consumers and companies
that are signed up to our scheme. Our service is free,
impartial and simple to use.

Before you can submit a complaint to us you should have:

  • Logged a formal complaint with your provider.
  • with your provider to resolve the complaint.
  • Received a deadlock letter or not received a
    satisfactory resolution after 8 weeks (6 weeks for
    energy complaints about SSE).
  • Gathered any evidence you have.
